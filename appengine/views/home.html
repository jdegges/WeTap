WeTap is a participatory sensing campaign geared towards identifying, describing and mapping public drinking fountains.

This service helps provide access to free water for those who simply want water to drink without waste or want to refill their reusable canteens while out of the home. With this Android application you can view a map of nearby drinking water sources as well as rate specific fountains and assist in helping this vital public infrastructure improve.
